ABOUT US & THE ARTIST

The Story Behind Akarshan

Akarshan Bracelets began as a passion project in a small studio, driven by the desire to create meaningful, handcrafted pieces that tell a story. Each bracelet is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who pour their heart and soul into every detail.

Meet the Artist

Our founder and lead designer, Miss. Neha Yadav, discovered her love for bracelet making during her travels through rural India. Inspired by traditional craftsmanship and modern aesthetics, she began creating pieces that blend cultural heritage with contemporary design.

"Every bracelet I create carries a piece of my journey. I believe in jewelry that not only adorns but also empowers – each piece is designed to make the wearer feel confident, beautiful, and connected to something meaningful." - Neha
